Welcome to Clinicians Club of Meru University-CCMU

VISION

To have competent,innovative and marketable clinical officers globally.

MISSION

We strive to nurture and mentor clinical officers with competitiveness and holistic services in respect to our professionalism.

Objectives

1.To carry out community diagnosis through community participation and sensitization.

2.To organize academic and extra-curriculum activities for clinical medicine students

3.To speak for clinicians in matters of administration

4.To create a forum for clinicians to give back to the society in terms of service such as free medical camps.

5.To create a uniting forum for all clinicians

6.To provide mentorship amongst ourselves and organize for the same from relevant sources

7.To solicit for sponsorship and bursaries to the needy students within clinical medicine course.

8.To facilitate for strong and active alumni program within the scope of the fraternity

9.To establish a forum and formula for working in synergy with other organization within and out of the University
